Currency Converter

Description

Currency Converter was made to convert currency from IDR to six other currency, listed below:

  • Taiwan - $ NTD (New Taiwan Dollar)
  • United States - $ USD (America Dollar)
  • Japan - ¥ JPY (Japanese Yen)
  • China - ¥ CNY (Chinese Yuan)
  • Europe - € Euro (Europe Euro)
  • British - £ Poundsterling (British Poundsterling)
